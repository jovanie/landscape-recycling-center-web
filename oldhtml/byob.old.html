B.Y.O.B. Punch Card Program

Get up to 42 five-gallon buckets of LRC product for $30!

The Landscape Recycling Center is replacing its previous compost bucket program with a new punch card system that includes an expanded line-up of LRC products. For just $30 per card, you can:

  • Bring your own five-gallon buckets to our site
  • Fill up to 7 buckets with product per punch (maximum of 42 buckets per card)
  • Choose from garden compost, premium shred mulch, premium chip mulch, standard shred mulch, select chip mulch, or stable-to-soil enhancer. (cannot be used for pulverized topsoil)

Card expires 12/31/19

Punch Card Rules
Drop off card at ticket booth prior to filling buckets. Once buckets are filled, stop by booth to punch out and retrieve card.

Cannot be used to fill vehicles. Applies to five-gallon buckets only.
