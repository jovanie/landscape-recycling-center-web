Mulching Tips
Premium Shredded Hardwood Mulch
For trees, shrubs, perennials, annual flower and spring flowering bulbs
Apply mulch no more than 2 inches deep

Premium Chipped Hardwood Mulch
For pathways, garden trails, trees, shrubs, perennials, annual flowers and spring flowering bulbs
Apply mulch no more than 4 inches deep
