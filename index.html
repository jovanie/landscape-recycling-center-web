Welcome!

We are a not-for-profit facility that turns local yard debris such as grass clippings, brush and plant cuttings into useful mulches and compost for the home and garden. Other products such as granite fines, asphalt millings and street pavers are materials that have been specifically salvaged for recycling. All of our products are for sale to residents, landscapers, nurseries and other businesses.
