Welcome!

LRC recycles local yard debris such as grass clippings, brush and plant cuttings into useful mulches and composts for the home and garden. We also offer salvaged landscaping products such as granite fines, asphalt millings and street pavers when available. Products are for sale to residents, landscapers, nurseries and businesses. Revenue is used to help offset the cost of operating the not-for-profit community facility.
