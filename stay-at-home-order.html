CLOSED DURING STAY-AT-HOME ORDER

Effective Date: March 21, 2020 - TBD
Hours: CLOSED

Pursuant to the Governor’s stay-at-home order, LRC is closed to the public effective 4 pm on Saturday, March 21.

LRC will complete scheduled deliveries for the week of 3/23, but we will not take any further delivery orders at this time.

Please follow the Governor’s and the Champaign-Urbana Public Health District’s health recommendations. We’ll be doing the same.


Update: March 30, 2020

We intend to have a phased-in re-opening of LRC over the next couple of weeks. We are working on some procedures at the ticket booth to provide more protection for employees and our customers.

Over the past two weeks, top priority was given to transitioning dozens of city employees to working remotely and establishing new operational procedures for maintaining core Public Works functions.

This week we were able to turn our attention to Landscape Recycling Center operations. We appreciate the public's patience during this very difficult time as we work to slow the spread of COVID19 and maintain core city functions.
