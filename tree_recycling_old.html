Free Christmas Tree Recycling

Now through February 28th, 2019.

Give your Christmas tree a new purpose after the holidays. Tree recycling at LRC turns ground up trees into acidifier mulch which can later be used for planting in the spring.

Trees must be clean and free of all decorations, tinsel, garland, wires, lights, tree stands, tree bags, tags, or any other extraneous material.

Flocked trees are not recyclable and will not be accepted at LRC.

Offer for county residents only and excludes commercial businesses, tree growers, and retailers. Clean Christmas trees are otherwise accepted for the regular free of $9.00 per cubic yard.
